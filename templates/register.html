🔒 HIPAA Privacy Notice

Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act

AutexAI Care is committed to protecting your health information in compliance with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) of 1996.

1. What Information We Collect

  • Your name, email address, and account credentials
  • Behavioral screening responses about your child
  • ASD screening results and probability scores
  • Appointment booking details with healthcare providers
  • Device information and usage logs for security purposes

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide ASD early screening services and generate reports
  • To facilitate appointment booking with recommended specialists
  • To improve the accuracy of our AI screening model
  • To send appointment confirmation and reminder emails
  • We never sell your personal or health information to third parties

3. Data Security & Encryption

  • All data is transmitted over SSL/TLS encrypted connections
  • Passwords are stored as cryptographic hashes (never plain text)
  • Database access is restricted and access-logged
  • Session tokens are signed and expire automatically

4. Your Rights Under HIPAA

  • Right to Access: You may request a copy of your health information at any time
  • Right to Correct: You may request correction of inaccurate information
  • Right to Delete: You may request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Right to Restrict: You may limit how your information is used
  • Right to Complain: You may file a complaint without retaliation

5. Disclosure to Third Parties

We may share information only in these situations:

  • With healthcare providers you explicitly choose to consult
  • When required by law or court order
  • In anonymized, aggregated form for research (no personal identifiers)

6. Data Retention

  • Account data is retained while your account is active
  • Screening reports are stored for 12 months by default
  • You may request immediate deletion at any time

7. Screening Disclaimer

⚠️ Important: AutexAI Care is a screening tool only and does NOT provide medical diagnosis. Results should always be reviewed by a qualified healthcare professional. This platform is not a substitute for professional medical advice.
